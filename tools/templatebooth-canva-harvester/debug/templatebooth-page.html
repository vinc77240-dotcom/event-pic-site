Black Gold Party Photo Booth Template

  • Birthday Photo Booth Templates
  • 12/04/2026

Celebrate in Style with Black, Gold & Glam – The Perfect Photo Booth Template for Luxe Parties and Events!

The Black Gold Party Photo Booth Template features an elegant design with shimmering gold accents and bold black tones.

Perfectly suited for milestone celebrations, luxurious birthday parties, or sophisticated gala nights, this template brings a touch of class to any event.

This set includes layouts for 2×6 photo strips, portrait and landscape 4×6 postcards, matching animated overlays, and animated welcome screens.

Available in DSLR Booth, LumaBooth, Darkroom Booth formats, as well as Photoshop PSD, Canva, and Clipart PNG files, this versatile template is compatible with leading photo booth software.

Pair modern elegance with timeless flair to deliver unforgettable keepsakes your clients will cherish!
