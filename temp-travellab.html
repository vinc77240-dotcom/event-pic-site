THIS IS OUR STORY

TravelLab is a technology and fintech group disrupting the travel eco system in Africa

The TravelLab Group is Africa’s leading travel and fintech technology group.

Our journey began in Sweden over 20 years ago as a pioneer in a nascent OTA industry; 
with one goal – making travel easier
Today, TravelLab serves the African market from its hub in Cape Town with a diversified portfolio of brands; featuring market leaders in various travel, fintech and technology verticals, geographic markets, distribution models, customer segments, and travel products.

With over two decades of travel and technology experience, we are committed to disrupting travel and adjacent financial market through innovative technology that captures people’s hearts.

why we love africa

The African Internet economy is one of the largest overlooked opportunities of the past decade.

The mobile Internet is transforming life across the continent with the support of growing local connectivity and mobility and a dynamic, young urban population.

The Internet economy is improving productivity and efficiencies across large swaths of the economy.

Africa has the world’s youngest, fastest-growing, and increasingly urbanized workforce. These demographics, coupled with improved longevity and education levels, have led to a rise in the consumption of online services.

Additionally, heavily concentrated populations in Africa’s cities are supporting developer communities that attract new talent pools.

A robust collaboration between the private and public sectors, between startups/scaleups and established blue chip companies are becoming the new normal which drives innovation and lowers entry costs.
